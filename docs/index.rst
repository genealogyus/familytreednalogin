Security Tips for FamilyTreeDNA Login
-------------------------------------

Protecting your FamilyTreeDNA Login account is important because it contains personal genetic information.

Create a strong password that combines letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using easily guessed personal information such as birthdays or names.

Never share your login credentials with other people. Even trusted individuals should only access your account with your permission.

Use secure internet connections whenever possible. Avoid logging into your account through unsecured public wireless networks.

Regularly review your account settings and update your password if you believe your account security may have been compromised.
